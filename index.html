NEW YORK NEW YORK!

NYC is alive 24 hours a day. There's not a single hour where the city isn't vibrant and alive. If you ever decide to visit, you must explore the city at all hours, including 3 AM in Korea Town. You may have seen the living spaces in New York be extremely small, sometimes the size of a walk-in closet. This is how coveted living in New York City is! You don't need a place to live if you can walk outside right into the greatest party of your life! You will discover so many things about New York City and learn so much about yourself as you live and explore this concrete jungle. If you came from a small town, a suburb, or under a rock then you NEED to make your way to NYC!
